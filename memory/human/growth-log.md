## 2026-04 — Grantee Crisis Management
First experience handling a key-person departure (LearnTrail CTO). Learned: 90-day runway with a hard gate is better than immediate termination or open-ended support. Rewarding transparency (Elena was upfront) builds trust across the portfolio. Other grantees notice how you treat struggling grantees.

## 2026-04 — Standards Body Diplomacy
Learned that standards alignment works better through bridge specifications than by picking winners. Both Linda Zhang (1EdTech) and David Kowalski (W3C) respond to "your work is respected, we want to connect it" better than "the field needs to converge." Frame as enabling, not replacing.

## 2026-Q1 — First-Time PI Mentoring
Marcus Rivera (OpenSync) needed grant management guidance, not technical guidance. Learned: first-time grant recipients often over-document technical details and under-document outcomes in reports. A 20-minute walkthrough of what funders actually read saves months of miscommunication.
