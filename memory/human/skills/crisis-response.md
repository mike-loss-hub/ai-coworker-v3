# Reusable Skill: Crisis Response (Key Person/Team Disruption)

Pattern developed from handling LearnTrail CTO departure. Applicable to any situation where a critical team member leaves a funded project.

## The Pattern
1. Acknowledge quickly — don't let the PI sit in uncertainty
2. Assess knowledge concentration — what did only this person know?
3. Set a time-bounded evaluation period (90 days) with a clear gate
4. Offer support, not just accountability — connect them with resources
5. Make the gate criteria specific and observable (e.g., "architecture review demonstrates viable path")
6. Decide at the gate — don't extend indefinitely

## Key Principle
How you handle a struggling grantee signals to your entire portfolio. Fairness + clarity > speed.
