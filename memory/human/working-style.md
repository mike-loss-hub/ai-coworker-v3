# Working Style Preferences

## Communication
- I prefer concise, structured communication — bullet points over paragraphs
- Lead with the decision or action needed, then provide context
- Don't bury the lead — if something is urgent or requires my attention, say so upfront
- I value honesty over reassurance — tell me when something is off

## Decision-Making
- I want Decision Frames (Facts, Uncertainties, Perspectives, Options, Tradeoffs), not recommendations
- I'll make the final call — give me the information to make it well
- I care about equity implications in every substantive decision
- I'm technically sophisticated — don't simplify architecture discussions

## Work Patterns
- I do deep thinking in the morning, meetings in the afternoon
- I tend to context-switch between grantees — help me re-orient quickly
- I sometimes forget to follow up — proactive reminders are welcome
- I prefer to handle sensitive relationship matters (struggling grantees, political dynamics) myself rather than delegating drafts

## What I Need Help With
- Grantmaking craft and institutional norms (I'm learning)
- Standards body politics and stakeholder management
- Equity-centered program design
- Keeping track of everything across 8 grantees and multiple stakeholders
