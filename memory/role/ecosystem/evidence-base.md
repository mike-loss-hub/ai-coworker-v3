# Evidence Base

**Last Updated:** 2026-04-28

## Key Research Findings

### Credential Portability
- **AACRAO 2025 Survey:** 73% of registrars report "significant friction" in credential transfer between institutions. Average transfer evaluation takes 4-6 weeks. Students lose 13 credits on average during transfer.
- **Lumina/Credential Engine 2025:** Credential transparency efforts have catalogued 1.1M+ credentials. But discovery ≠ portability — credentials are findable but not transferable.

### AI in Education
- **EDUCAUSE 2026 Horizon Report:** AI advising agents deployed at 35% of 4-year institutions, but <5% at community colleges. Context about the learner is the #1 requested feature by advisors.
- **Digital Promise 2025 (Asante et al.):** AI tutoring systems show 15% improvement in course completion, but gains are concentrated among students who already have strong digital literacy. Equity gap widens without deliberate design.

### Local-First & Offline Access
- **Pew Research 2025:** 24% of rural Americans lack reliable broadband. For community college students in rural areas, 31% report "frequent connectivity issues" affecting coursework.
- **NCES 2025:** 18% of community college students share a computing device with 2+ other people. Device-sharing models are essential, not edge cases.

### Standards Adoption
- **IMS Global 2025 Adoption Survey:** CLR adoption growing at 40% year-over-year in member institutions, but primarily at well-resourced 4-year universities. Community college adoption is <10%.
- **W3C VC Adoption Tracker:** VC issuance up 300% since 2024, driven primarily by workforce credentialing (not education). Education sector is lagging.

### Multi-Agent Coordination
- **No direct precedent in education.** Multi-agent coordination research exists in robotics and enterprise AI but has not been applied to learner-facing systems. Our PathWeaver grant is one of the first.

## Evidence Gaps
1. No longitudinal studies on learner context portability outcomes (our portfolio will be among the first)
2. Limited research on learner attitudes toward AI-managed context (privacy, trust, control)
3. No equity-disaggregated data on credential portability benefits — FieldKit pilot will address this
4. Insufficient evidence on CRDT reliability for credential-grade data at scale — LearnChain and OpenSync will generate this
