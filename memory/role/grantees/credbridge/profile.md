## Project Summary
CredBridge is developing a provenance-tracking layer for AI-generated credential summaries. When an AI agent summarizes a learner's credential history, the system attaches cryptographic provenance metadata that links each claim back to its source credential. This enables downstream systems to verify not just that a claim was made, but where the information originated. Currently 6 weeks behind schedule — the credential verification pipeline was more complex than anticipated.
