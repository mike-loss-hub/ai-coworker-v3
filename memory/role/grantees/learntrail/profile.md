## Project Summary
LearnTrail is building provenance infrastructure for tracing AI-generated learner summaries back to their source observations and credentials. When an AI agent says "this learner demonstrates strong quantitative reasoning," the provenance graph shows which specific assessments, course completions, and instructor observations support that claim. Critical for trust in AI-mediated credential systems.
