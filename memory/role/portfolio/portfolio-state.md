## Grant Narratives

### LearnChain Labs — 🟢 Green
Building a CRDT-based longitudinal learning record store that synchronizes learner context across institutions. Year 2 focus is performance at scale — can the system handle 10K+ concurrent learners with acceptable merge latency? Early results promising. Strong team, good communication. PI Amara Osei is collaborative and transparent about challenges.

### CredBridge Institute — 🟡 Yellow
Developing a provenance-tracking layer for AI-generated credential summaries. Flagged yellow because they're 6 weeks behind on their credential verification pipeline — scope was more complex than anticipated. PI Raj Patel has submitted a revised timeline for review. Technical approach is sound but team is stretched thin. Need to assess whether timeline slip affects downstream dependencies.

### PathWeaver AI — 🟢 Green
Building reference implementation for multi-agent coordination around shared learner records. Using MCP protocol as the transport layer. Year 2 demo will show 3 agents (advising, tutoring, career) coordinating around a single learner profile without data duplication. Sofia Chen runs a tight ship. Good standards body relationships.

### OpenSync Foundation — 🟢 Green
CRDT-based sync protocol for offline-first learner data. Marcus Rivera's first major grant — he's a strong CRDT researcher from the local-first community. Clean architecture, good documentation. On track for v0.3 which adds conflict resolution for credential-grade data.

### LearnTrail Research — 🔴 Red
Provenance architecture for tracing AI-generated learner summaries back to source observations. Flagged RED: CTO Dr. Kevin Park departed in March — he was the only person who fully understood the provenance graph architecture. PI Elena Vasquez is capable but needs to either recruit a replacement or simplify the architecture. Decision made April 15 to provide 90-day support runway. Watching closely.

### TrustLayer Technologies — 🟢 Green
Building trust framework that bridges 1EdTech and W3C credential verification. James Okafor's experience at 1EdTech gives him unique insight into both ecosystems. Year 2 deliverable is a formal specification for cross-standard trust anchors. On track.

### EdgeWise Computing — 🟡 Yellow
Edge inference engine for running learner context models on low-resource devices. Yellow because initial benchmarks on Chromebook-class hardware showed higher latency than projected. PI Priya Sharma is investigating model quantization approaches. Technical risk is manageable but needs monitoring.

### FieldKit Education — 🟢 Green
Integrated learner memory + offline-capable system designed for rural community colleges. Tomás Gutierrez brings deep understanding of the target population. Piloting with 3 community colleges in the Central Valley. Hardware procurement complete, software integration on schedule for June pilot launch.
