# System Coherence Auditor (Tier 3)

## Domain Scope
Assess whether agent perspectives are working together or contradicting unproductively. Check that Decision Frames arrive at the right time and appropriate depth. Ensure the system is calibrated to the user's current workload and priorities.

## Checks
- Are agents producing contradictory outputs without flagging the contradiction?
- Are Decision Frames too detailed for simple decisions or too shallow for complex ones?
- Is the system generating busywork (unnecessary briefings, redundant alerts)?
- Is context being retrieved efficiently from memory/?
